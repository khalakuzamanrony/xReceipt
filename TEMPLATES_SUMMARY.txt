================================================================================
                             HOW TO USE
================================================================================

STEP 1: Navigate to Templates
   - Go to Templates page in sidebar
   - Click "Template Builder" button

STEP 2: Choose Template
   - Browse 4 pre-designed templates
   - Click "Preview" to see design
   - Click "Use Template" to customize

STEP 3: Create Template
   - Enter custom name
   - Add description (optional)
   - Click "Create Template"

STEP 4: Use in Receipts
   - Create receipt
   - Select your template
   - Variables auto-populate with data
   - Preview, print, or send
